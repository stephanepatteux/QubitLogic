## About QubitLogic

QubitLogic is a technical blog covering **quantum computing**, **AI infrastructure**, and **Python development** — with a focus on honest benchmarks, reproducible code, and practical implementation.

Every article is written with working code you can run. Benchmark numbers come from real hardware and are reproducible with the commands provided. Affiliate relationships do not influence verdicts — see the [Affiliate Disclosure](/affiliate-disclosure/).

---

## The Series Structure

The site is organised as three linked phases, each building on the last:

| Phase | Section | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [Infrastructure](/infrastructure/) | VPS provisioning, Nginx, Python, CI/CD |
| 2 | [Quantum Coding](/quantum-coding/) | Qiskit, QAOA, Grover's, hybrid ML |
| 3 | [Professional Edge](/professional-edge/) | PQC, RAG agents, financial APIs, compliance |

The VPS provisioned in Phase 1 runs the quantum experiments in Phase 2. The security tooling in Phase 3 protects the APIs built throughout.
